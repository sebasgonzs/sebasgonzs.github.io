CHINA

China is a country in East Asia. With a population exceeding 1.4 billion, it is the world's second-most populous country after India, representing 17.4% of the world population. China spans the equivalent of five time zones and borders fourteen countries by land. Beijing is the country's capital, while Shanghai is its most populous city by urban area and largest financial center.


Places

GREAT WALL OF CHINA

The Great Wall of China is the longest man-made structure ever designed or built in history. The Great Wall of China was built to serve as defense against enemy attack from nomadic tribes north of China. It also served to protect the trade route across China.



FORBIDDEN CITY

A famous tourist attraction in Beijing that was the winter residence for emperors from the Ming to the Qing dynasties for over 500 years



Shanghai

China's commercial center and most cosmopolitan city, with a special economic zone called Pudong
